Mercedes-AMG GT R

The Mercedes-AMG GT R is a high-performance sports car designed for maximum performance on both the racetrack and the road. It is powered by a 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine producing approximately 577 hp. Its aerodynamic design includes a large rear spoiler and the signature "Panamericana" front grille for optimal airflow.

Rp. 5.500.000.000,00

Lamborghini Aventador

The Lamborghini Aventador is a mid-engine sports car known for its aggressive, sharp, and futuristic design. It features a powerful V12 engine, capable of accelerating from 0 to 100 km/h in just seconds. Throughout its production run, the model has become a symbol of luxury and high performance, with variants including the Aventador S, SVJ, and its final edition, the LP 780-4 Ultimae.

Rp. 18.000.000.000,00

Porsche 718 Cayman

The Porsche 718 Cayman is a two-door sports car designed to deliver a pure driving experience. It is equipped with a flat-four or flat-six engine (depending on the variant) that is mid-mounted for optimal weight distribution. Its sleek design with iconic side air intakes makes it one of the most agile sports cars in its class, perfectly suited for everyday use and the racetrack.

Rp. 2.500.000.000,00

McLaren 650S

The McLaren 650S is a high-performance sports car powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine. It is known for its use of a carbon-fiber-reinforced polymer chassis, making it extremely light and strong. Its front end, inspired by the McLaren P1 hypercar, provides an aggressive appearance and optimal aerodynamics.

Rp. 4.490.000.000,00
